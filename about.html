As we celebrate our 10th year in business, we thought it worthwhile to share our thoughts on the past quarter century.It is hard to believe that it has been ten years. Who would have predicted that a hobby a passion of one person would survive to celebrate its 10thyear in business? We started as a one-man army at the beginning. Some would say our survival is a tribute to our hard work. We like to think that our 10 years of success can be attributed to a meticulous commitment to our core values – “Our Commitment is to Excellence”

This commitment provides us an opportunity to both reflect on our past and to look forward to our future. As we celebrate, we like to say “Thank You!” To our clients: We are so appreciative and honoured to work with our clients. We would like to thank our clients for their belief in us and our abilities to help them achieve their goals and vision of success, while always challenging us to be better. Our team is continuously enriched by the people that we’ve met and worked with at our client organizations. Many of these people have also become friends and advisors, allowing us to grow and learn individually, and, as a company. Thank you for your unwavering commitment and great relationships. “Thank You!” To our Vendors & partners: We would also like to thank all our Vendors, suppliers, partnering companies and global partners we collaborate with to deliver incredible work that makes a positive, sustainable impact on our client businesses. We are proud to have been working /associated with you. Thank you for a special 10 years and here’s to celebrating the next successful chapter together. “Thank You!” To our team members: To be in business for 10 years requires having incredible people in your organization. We are so grateful for the opportunity to work every day with a group of talented, passionate and caring people. Your willingness to give of yourselves and help each other is very special and is deeply admired. You are the Best! We ‘d like this “Thank you!” to reach out all who were and are with us! Together we have established a track record of doing the impossible. At the heart of these accomplishments is a great deal of hard work, dedication, and a willingness to take risks to benefit our customers. Although we may be tempted to indulge in a self-congratulatory moment on our 10th anniversary, we remain mindful that the world is an ever-changing place, and if anything, we must accelerate, not relax. We are already thinking ahead to what the next 10years & more will hold. What will the IT industry look like? One wonders what innovative designs and engineering challenges there might be. We have come a long way; our work is not done! Building upon our industry leadership and expertise requires further dedication, commitment to customers, innovation, and execution of top priorities. Two of our priorities continue to be: 1. Growth and expansion of our business by increasing the bar for the highest quality service in the industry. 2. Achieving technological leadership in our industry. Our culture of passion for delivering customer solutions is unique. Despite the many changes we will undoubtedly make, it is this passion, and our relationships with you, our customers! our clients! our partners! our vendors! our team! our well-wishers & friends, which will remain the foundation for our next 25 & more years. For this we thank you, and look forward to your continued support. Always Believe in Getting Better …
